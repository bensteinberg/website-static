The Library Innovation Lab is preparing to launch the Institutional Data Initiative (IDI), a new initiative to unlock and refine high quality training data at library, academic, and government institutions across the world.

By bridging the gap between model-makers and institutions through a world-class data practice, IDI stands to increase access to knowledge, empower contributing institutions and the cultures they represent, and advance the state of the art for all builders of AI.

The Lab is currently building partnerships with institutions and model-makers ahead of a public launch. Reach out to find out more. {{ site.email }}
