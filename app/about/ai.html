The Library Innovation Lab is bringing together experts who are driving the development, adoption, and regulation of AI technology as it rewires society and the law along with it.


This event is invitation only. If you have received an invitation but have not yet filled out an RSVP / attendee information form, please do so at your earliest convenience.

Where and When

The Harvard Law AI Summit will take place September 19, 2023 at the Harvard Law School in Cambridge, Massachusetts. Breakfast and registration will begin at 9am, programming will run from 10am-4pm, and a reception will take place from 5-8pm.

Directions to Campus

The Summit will be held in the Reginald F. Lewis Law Center on the Harvard Law School campus. The street address is 1557 Massachusetts Avenue, Cambridge, MA 02138.

You can find directions to campus via your favorite mode of transportation on the HLS website.

If you plan to park on campus for the gathering and have not already let us know please contact Harmony Eidolon to arrange a space.

Suggested Readings

  • Richard Susskind's "Some Thoughts on AI in the Law," via his newsletter, July 2023.
  • Judge Brantley Starr's Mandatory Certification Regarding Generative Artificial Intelligence, from the US District Court for the Northern District of Texas, May 2023.
  • Stephanie Pacheco's "ANALYSIS: AI Is Making a Summer Splash in Legal," from Bloomberg Law, August 2023.
  • Bruce Schneier And Nathan E. Sanders' "Six Ways That AI Could Change Politics" from MIT Technology Review, July 2023.
