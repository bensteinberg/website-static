Who We Are

The Library Innovation Lab is a team working at the intersection of libraries, technology, and law.

Our team combines librarians, technologists, lawyers, and more to build tools and conduct research to empower more people to access and build our shared knowledge and cultural heritage.

{% include arrow-link.html label="Learn More About Who We Are" href="/about" %}

Our Work

We build and explore new technology through the lens of library principles. Perma.cc, a web preservation and citation tool; H2O, an open casebook authoring and reading platform; and The Caselaw Access Project, an open database of over 6 million court opinions free and open to the public.

We also conduct research to further our understanding of certain technologies and create guides for others working in our field.
