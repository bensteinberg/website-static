Research

Research at LIL is structured around a variety of modes and outcomes, though it all ultimately serves our goal of fusing the library world with new technologies. We use our research to understand new - or new to us - communities and their extant work around a knowledge challenge; to tackle a particularly thorny and consequential technical problem; or to work with libraries to map the technical possibilities available to them for a particular part of their mission.

Some of our research is explicitly tied to our ongoing tentpole projects, while other explorations may become larger projects down the line.
